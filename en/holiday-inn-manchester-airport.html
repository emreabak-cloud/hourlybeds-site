About CitizenM Manchester Piccadilly

Perfectly situated in the vibrant heart of Manchester near Piccadilly Station, citizenM Manchester Piccadilly combines a bold, tech-forward boutique design with high-end comfort. Tailored for modern corporate travelers and international transit guests looking for premium micro-stays, the hotel features wall-to-wall windows, full smart-room automation via MoodPads, ultra-comfortable XL king-size beds, and free ultra-fast Wi-Fi. It provides an efficient and stylish environment to rest or work productively by the hour.

Manchester'ın hareketli merkezinde, Piccadilly İstasyonu'na yürüme mesafesinde konumlanan citizenM Manchester Piccadilly, cesur ve teknoloji odaklı butik tasarımını üst düzey konforla birleştirir. Premium mikro konaklamalar arayan modern kurumsal profesyoneller ve uluslararası transit yolcular için optimize edilen tesiste; boydan boya pencereler, MoodPad tabletler üzerinden tam oda otomasyonu, ultra konforlu XL büyük boy yataklar ve ücretsiz ultra hızlı Wi-Fi yer almaktadır. Saatlik bazda dinlenmek veya verimli çalışmak için şık ve fonksiyonel bir ortam sunar.

Frequently Asked Questions

Cancellation is entirely free of charge until 24 hours before your scheduled check-in time. You can cancel your reservation through the following ways:
  • Via your booking confirmation email
  • By logging into your User Dashboard
Service and management fees will be automatically refunded; please note that it may take 5 to 10 business days for the refund to reflect on your account. In the event of a cancellation made less than 24 hours prior to check-in, the hotel reserves the right to charge the full amount of the room rate, even if the room is not used.

Since your reservation is managed through HourlyBeds, amendment requests can only be processed by our team. Your request will be immediately forwarded to the hotel. Please keep in mind that modifications are subject to hotel availability and policies, and cannot always be guaranteed.

When booking with us, you only pay the service/management fee online. The remaining balance for the room will be settled directly at the hotel during check-in, using your preferred payment method. Please note that certain hotels may require full payment at the time of booking. If applicable, this will be clearly displayed on your screen right before completing your reservation.
